## **1. The Video Infrastructure (The "Heavy Lift")**

### **Google Drive (The Warehouse)**

- **The Concept:** Think of this as our secure, off-site storage facility. We only use this for our massive video project folders.
    
- **How it Works (The Sync):** Once I have the fiber internet, we’ll use the Google Drive desktop app. It makes the cloud look like a folder on our computers. When we work on a video, we "Check Out" the folder (Mark as Offline). We edit at full speed. When done, we "Check It In" (Online Only).
    
- **Why the Change?** Right now, we struggle to move 15GB files. This change allows us to share high-quality raw footage between Indiana and Texas without physically mailing hard drives.
    
- **Goal Growth:** By Shot Show 2027, you’ll have hundreds of hours of high-quality footage. This system ensures that footage is safe and searchable, not lost on a dying thumb drive.
    

### **Blackmagic Cloud (The Global Control Room)**

- **The Concept:** This is the "brain" of the edit. If Google Drive holds the "parts" (the footage), Blackmagic Cloud holds the "instructions" (the project file).
    
- **The Example:** I can start an edit in Indiana, and you can open that **exact same project** in Texas. You can watch what I’ve done, add notes, or even swap out a clip.
    
- **Future Training Goal:** When you transition to a training company, you’ll be filming drills. I can be editing the "Instructional Video" in real-time while you’re still on the range filming the next segment.
