### **Categorized Items**

#### **Content Production & Asset Management**

- **Air.inc:** A visual Digital Asset Management (DAM) tool for organizing and reviewing videos with AI tagging.
    
- **DaVinci Resolve Studio:** Professional video/audio editing and AI voice conversion.
    
- **OpusClip Pro:** AI video clipping and automated captions.
    
- **Stable Diffusion Forge:** Local AI image generator (RTX 4070 powered).
    
- **GPT-SoVITS:** Local AI voice cloning for the Sarge voice.
    
- **Envato Elements:** Unlimited stock assets and templates.
    
- **Four Editors Bundle:** One-time purchase of 10,000+ editing presets.
    
- **Epidemic Sound:** Copyright-safe music and SFX.
    
- **NVIDIA Broadcast:** AI-powered hardware cleanup for mic/camera.
    
- **ElevenLabs:** High-end AI voice generation (Backup/Optional).

- **Speechify:** AI text-to-speech reading tool.
