#### **II. Why Iceland is "Better" for the Brand**

In the US, even if you label your content, you can still be sued by a private individual under "Public Nuisance" or "Intellectual Property" theories that vary by state.

The primary benefit of Iceland is that it removes the brand from the US **civil court system** regarding AI. If a US activist wants to claim your AI-generated training images are "misleading," they cannot easily use a US state law to shut down an Icelandic server.
