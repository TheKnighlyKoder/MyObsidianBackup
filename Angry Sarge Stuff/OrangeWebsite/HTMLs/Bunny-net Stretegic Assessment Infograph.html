2. Jurisdictional Radar

Slovenia vs. USA vs. Iceland: A tactical comparison of legal terrain.

Slovenia offers a "Sweet Spot" between US performance and Icelandic security. While Iceland is the ultimate fortress for anti-doxxing (IMMI laws), its physical distance creates latency. Slovenia provides strong GDPR/DSA shielding against US civil subpoenas while maintaining EU connectivity. The USA remains superior for speed but poses maximum risk for de-platforming.

Key Intelligence:

  • DSA (Slovenia): Distinguishes "Harmful" vs "Illegal". Firearm assembly is generally "Harmful" (allowed) not "Illegal" (banned).
  • GDPR (Slovenia): Server logs are PII. Protects user metadata from US civil discovery.
  • IMMI (Iceland): Provides specific "Anti-Subpoena" protections for whistleblowers.
