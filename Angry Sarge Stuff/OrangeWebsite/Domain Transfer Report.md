### **2. Jurisdictional Advantage: The "Icelandic Firewall"**

Unlike US-based registrars (e.g., GoDaddy, SiteGround), OrangeWebsite operates under the **Modern Media Initiative (IMMI)** and the **Act No. 90/2018 on Data Protection**.

- **Immunity to US Civil Subpoenas:** As a registrar based in Reykjavik, OrangeWebsite does not recognize US civil subpoenas or "private right of action" notices (common in CA AB 1594 cases).
    
- **Dual Illegality Requirement:** To compel the release of domain ownership data, a third party must prove a violation of **Icelandic law**. Since 2A editorial content and community discussion are legal in Iceland, foreign civil litigants have no domestic mechanism to enforce "discovery" of the brand's identity or assets.
    
- **Protection Against "Domain Seizure":** US registrars must comply with federal and state court orders to lock or seize domains. OrangeWebsite only adheres to Icelandic court orders, providing a barrier against the "administrative takedowns" often used against 2A brands.
